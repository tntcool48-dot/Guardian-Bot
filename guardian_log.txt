v1.4.0 - The Anti-Lag & QoL Update

UI & Customization

    Tabbed GUI: Cleaned up the interface into separate tabs so it's not a massive wall of settings.

    Auto-Save: The bot actually remembers your settings now. No more re-typing your config every time you launch.

    Full Control: Added sliders for the kill-switch, stuck thresholds, and row distances. You can also change your attack bind (no longer forced to use 'j') and toggle any failsafe on or off.

Engine Fixes & Anti-Cheat

    Fixed False Alarms (Anti-Lag): If Hypixel lags mid-row, the bot won't instantly panic and trigger the distance alarm anymore. It waits up to 5 seconds for the server to catch up.

    Smarter Stuck Detection: Turning is way smoother. The engine now ignores minor X-axis sliding, so weird block hitboxes won't break your rows.

    New Death Recovery: Scrapped the return-trench idea. The bot now just waits for the hub to load and hits 'Z' to teleport straight back to the farm spawn.

    No More Discord Spam: If you alt-tab while the bot is trying to type /warp garden or /locraw, it instantly aborts. Your chats are safe.

    Watchdog Safety: Added a 10-second cooldown to the /locraw check so you don't get flagged for spamming commands during lag spikes. Wrapped all emergency triggers in thread locks so they don't overlap and crash the bot.
